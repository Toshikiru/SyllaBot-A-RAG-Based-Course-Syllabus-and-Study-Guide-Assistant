1. course description
information management covers the principles and practices of managing organizational information
resources. topics include database systems, data modeling, information architecture, records
management, knowledge management, information security, and enterprise information systems.
students will analyze real-world information management problems and propose solutions using
standard frameworks and tools.
2. learning outcomes
lo 1: design and implement relational database schemas using normalization techniques.
lo 2: apply information architecture principles to organize and structure enterprise data.
lo 3: evaluate information security risks and recommend appropriate controls.
lo 4: use sql and database tools to retrieve and manipulate organizational data.
